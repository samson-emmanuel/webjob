ENERGY PRICING

  • Energy pricing is volatile and there are different drivers that affect the price of power on a daily basis, but the main underlying factor are natural gas futures, at least in the ERCOT market. Other factors related to the customer must be considered as well, including location, start date and load factor. For a look at custom commercial or industrial energy pricing, please contact us at 972-462-8800 or via email at pricing@aldenenergy.com
